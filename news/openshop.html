Regarding Spot Shop Channel

News Article - Posted 27th March 2018

Today, along with Revision 400 of the Disconnect24 Channel, you may have noticed that we removed the Spot Shop Channel button from the menu.

You may be already wandering why this happened or you may even be mourning at the thought of it's sudden cancellation.

The Spot Shop Channel has been rebranded as The Open Shop Channel and will be a standalone project. This is so we can make the service as universal as possible, meaning that any WiiConnect24 replacement server will be able to use The Open Shop and publish their content to it without fears of the service management censoring or removing it.

We hope to relist this service in DC24 Channel so you can use it freely when development is finished.
