Why REV and not VER?

Trivial Information - Posted 27th March 2018

A lot of software that you see usually identifies themselves with a term called Versions.

"Why doesn't DC24 Channel use the revisional system to identify itself instead of versions?" You may be asking.

Have you every read a textbook from a library about science or a dictionary in an English class? Educational materials tend to be revised and rewritten after certain periods of time, and usually are given a "revision number". These numbers indicate that the information has been updated and can be used to distingush an obsolete and up-to-date document.

DC24 Channel uses REV to distingush the HTML package documents.
